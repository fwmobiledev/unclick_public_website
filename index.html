Unlick is the only app of its kind that allows you to seek consent before you share photos on social network.So, if you are in the habit of posting a lot of photos of friends and family on Facebook or other social networks, Unclick lets you be kind to all your friends who care about how they look in photos, who they are with, where the photo was taken et al. So if you want to avoid a social faux pas of sharing an “oops” photo by mistake, you need Unclick. Now! 

USE WITH EASE

Select the photos that you want to share on your social network and select friends that you’d like to seek consent from. Easily view their responses to know which photos are objectionable and those that are good to share.

SEND PHOTOS SECURELY

The photos from an Unclick album are sent, in an encrypted format and are available only within the Unclick app. Once your album expires, your friends can no longer access it. In fact, the photos get deleted from their phones!

SHARE WITH CONFIDENCE

Unclick collates your friends’ responses and color tags the photos in your Unlick album – red or amber indicates the objectionable ones, and green are good to go. To share, simply use these tags to select photos from your Unclick album.
